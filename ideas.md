# Landing Page Design Ideas

## Approach A — Editorial Modernism
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets contemporary editorial design.

**Core Principles:**
- Strong typographic hierarchy drives all layout decisions
- Generous whitespace as a structural element, not a gap-filler
- Strict horizontal rule separators to delineate sections
- Content-first: imagery supports text, never competes

**Color Philosophy:**
- Off-white background (#F7F5F0) — warm, paper-like, not sterile
- Near-black ink (#1A1A18) for body text
- Single accent: deep terracotta (#C0533A) for CTAs and highlights
- Muted sage (#8FA68E) for secondary labels and team role tags

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric two-column grid: left-heavy content, right-side imagery
- Hero section uses oversized display type with a right-aligned portrait image
- Team section: horizontal scroll strip of cards on mobile, 4-col grid on desktop
- Contact section: full-width form with left label / right input alignment

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal rules (1px) between all major sections
- Oversized section numbers (01, 02, 03…) in light gray as decorative anchors
- Monospaced font for labels and metadata

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover on team cards lifts with a subtle shadow and reveals role description
- Navigation links underline on hover with a slow 300ms ease
- Form inputs have a bottom-border-only focus style (no box)

**Animation:**
- Sections fade-in and slide up 20px on scroll entry (staggered 80ms delay per element)
- No bouncy or springy animations — everything is measured and calm

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Playfair Display" — serif, high contrast, editorial authority
- Body: "Source Sans 3" — humanist sans, highly readable
- Labels/meta: "JetBrains Mono" — monospaced, technical precision
</text>

## Approach B — Warm Brutalism
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Brutalist web design softened with warm tones and tactile texture.

**Core Principles:**
- Raw, honest structure — borders and boxes are visible, not hidden
- Bold, unapologetic typography that commands attention
- Intentional imperfection: slight rotations, offset shadows, hand-drawn feel
- High contrast between sections via alternating background colors

**Color Philosophy:**
- Cream (#FDF6E3) and warm black (#1C1410) as the base pair
- Accent: vivid amber (#F5A623) for borders, highlights, and buttons
- Section alternation: cream → black → cream → amber strip
- No gradients — flat, solid fills only

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-width sections with thick top/bottom borders
- Hero: massive left-aligned headline, no hero image — pure type
- Team: grid of cards with thick black borders and offset drop shadows
- Location: split 50/50 — map placeholder left, address text right
- Contact: stacked form with large labeled inputs and a chunky submit button

**Signature Elements:**
- 3px solid black borders on all cards and interactive elements
- Offset box-shadows (4px 4px 0 black) on hover
- Uppercase, letter-spaced section labels

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states are immediate and bold — no slow transitions
- Buttons shift 2px on click (translateY) to simulate a physical press
- Focus rings are thick and visible

**Animation:**
- Minimal — only a fast 150ms ease-out on hover transforms
- No scroll-triggered animations; content is immediately visible

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Space Grotesk" — geometric, bold, modern brutalist
- Body: "DM Sans" — clean, neutral, readable
- Labels: "Space Mono" — monospaced, raw
</text>

## Approach C — Refined Minimal (Selected)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Contemporary Scandinavian minimalism — calm, purposeful, refined.

**Core Principles:**
- Every element earns its place; remove anything decorative that doesn't serve content
- Soft depth through subtle shadows and layered backgrounds, never flat
- Consistent 8px spacing grid throughout
- Mobile-first: sections stack gracefully and remain readable at any width

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: warm white (#FAFAF8)
- Surface: pure white (#FFFFFF) for cards
- Text: deep charcoal (#1E1E1E) primary, slate (#6B7280) secondary
- Accent: muted teal (#2D7D6F) — calm, professional, distinctive without being loud
- Border: very light gray (#E5E7EB)

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Single-column centered layout with max-width 1100px
- Hero: left-aligned headline + subtext, right-side abstract image (asymmetric split)
- Description: full-width with a subtle background tint and large pull-quote
- Team: 3-column card grid (1-col mobile, 2-col tablet, 3-col desktop)
- Location: two-column — map image left, address + hours right
- Contact: clean form, centered, max-width 600px

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin teal left-border accent on blockquotes and section intros
- Rounded avatar images with a soft ring border
- Subtle card hover: shadow deepens + slight upward translate

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- All transitions are 200–300ms ease-in-out
- Hover states are gentle — shadow and slight movement, no color explosions
- Form inputs have smooth focus ring in teal

**Animation:**
- Intersection Observer fade-in + translateY(16px → 0) for sections
- Staggered 60ms delay for team cards
- No looping or attention-grabbing animations

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Fraunces" — variable serif, warm and distinctive
- Body: "Nunito Sans" — friendly, highly legible sans-serif
- Labels: "Nunito Sans" semibold + uppercase + letter-spacing
</text>
